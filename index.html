Specialty Pharmacy

For chronic, complex or specialty medication needs.

Legacy’s specialty pharmacy supports patients with chronic and complex conditions such as cancer, Hepatitis C, HIV, rheumatoid arthritis and multiple sclerosis, as well as patients prescribed expensive medications. Some medications may require special handling such as refrigerated shipping.

Application pathDiscuss the program with a provider. A provider referral is needed.
Refill timingStaff contact patients 5 to 7 days before refills are due.
  • ✓Specially trained staff discuss treatment plans and answer questions.
  • ✓Patients are automatically enrolled in the Patient Management Program for training, education and counseling.
  • ✓The team collaborates with doctors and caregivers and coordinates prior authorization with insurance.
  • ✓Staff provide refill reminders and act as healthcare advocates during treatment.
  • ✓Delivery can be coordinated to a patient’s home, workplace or preferred location.
Concierge Pharmacy

For complex needs without specialty medications.

Legacy’s Concierge Pharmacy program offers an additional support level for patients with complex medical needs that require special knowledge, but who are not on specialty medications.

Application pathDiscuss the program with a provider. A provider referral is needed.
Refill timingStaff contact patients 5 to 7 days before refills are due.
  • ✓Staff discuss treatment plans and address questions or concerns.
  • ✓Patients are automatically enrolled in the Patient Management Program for training, education and counseling.
  • ✓The team collaborates directly with doctors and caregivers.
  • ✓Refill reminders and close follow-up help patients receive medications and supplies efficiently.
  • ✓Benefits support helps patients explore options and understand prescription and medical insurance benefits.

Contact & questions

Give patients answers before they call.

How do I transfer a prescription?

Call one of Legacy’s pharmacies and provide your current pharmacy information and an up-to-date medication list. Legacy says it will take care of the rest.

How do I request mail delivery?

If you have an active Legacy prescription, request mail delivery through MyHealth or by calling or visiting one of the pharmacies.

How do I apply for Specialty Pharmacy or Concierge Pharmacy?

Discuss the program with your provider. A provider referral is needed for these support programs.

When do support-program refill reminders happen?

Staff contact patients 5 to 7 days before refills are due to coordinate medications, records, delivery date and address.
